Biography

I'm a master student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Peng Cui. I visited Carnegie Mellon University in 2023 and was advised by Prof. Steven Wu. I got my Bachelor degree in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2022. My research is focused on machine learning, especially towards Out-of-Distribution Generalization (OOD) and Robust Optimization. I'm also interested in machine learning for biology. Here is my CV.

I will apply for PhD for 2025 fall.
